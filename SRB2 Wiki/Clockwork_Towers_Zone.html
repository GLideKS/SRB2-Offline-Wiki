MAPF5, Clockwork Towers Zone, is the sixth Capture the Flag level in Sonic Robo Blast 2 v2.2. It consists of two very large stone bases that are suspended in the sky and connected by a partially broken bridge. The stage was originally entered into the March/April 2010 OLDC, where it won with a score of 7.62.
