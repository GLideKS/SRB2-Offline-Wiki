MAPF3, Icicle Falls Zone, is the fourth Capture the Flag level in Sonic Robo Blast 2 v2.2. It is a very small ice-themed map, featuring slippery ice and a waterslide. The bases are fairly open and small, making this a fast-scoring stage. Despite its small size, it features three distinct paths from base to base. The level's name is a reference to Cirno's spell card "Icicle Fall" from Touhou Koumakyou 〜 the Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. Icicle Falls Zone was first featured in v2.0, but the bases were modified in v2.1, making it slightly harder to get to the flag.

Points of interest

Weapon rings

  • The Bounce Ring Panels are located in the base entrances.
  • The sole Rail Ring Panel is located on the bridge over the river in the top part of the level.
  • The Automatic Ring Panels are located between the bases and the central cave.
  • The Explosion Ring Panels are located on a platform on the ice outside each base.
  • The Scatter Ring Panels are located in the top part of the stage, above the cave entrances.
  • The sole Grenade Ring Panel is located in the central cave.

Monitors

  • The SRM is located on the large ice platform in the center of the lake. Its base item is a Force Shield.
  • One WRM each is located on the platforms that hold the Explosion Ring Panels. Its base item is a Super Sneakers Monitor.
  • One WRM each is located on the stairs that lead up to the top part of the stage. The WRM is flanked by two Super Ring Monitors. Its base item is a Super Sneakers Monitor.
