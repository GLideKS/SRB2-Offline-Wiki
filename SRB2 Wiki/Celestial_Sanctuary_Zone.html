MAPM9, Celestial Sanctuary Zone, is the tenth Match level in Sonic Robo Blast 2 v2.2. It takes place in an temple in the forest. This stage was originally entered in the November/December 2010 OLDC, where it placed second with a score of 7.00. The stage originally took place in an AGZ-themed sky temple, then in v2.2.14, it received design overhaul.
