MAPF1, Lost Palace Zone, is the second Capture the Flag level in Sonic Robo Blast 2 v2.2. Its theme is based on Aerial Garden Zone. Most of the stage consists of a large, multi-layered temple area floating above a death pit, with the bases extending to each side. The only way of accessing the flag from below is via red springs, which makes the bases a strong focus of action in the map. The stage was introduced in v1.08, but was slightly expanded and modified in v2.0 and received some additional decorations in v2.1. In v2.2, the level was given a redesign, being renamed from Cloud Palace to Lost Palace, and now taking place at night, while surrounded by water.

Points of interest

Weapon rings

  • The Bounce Ring Panels are located on the ledges that make up the middle layer of the stage, close to the centerpiece.
  • The Rail Ring Panels are located on the ledges that extend to the left of the main structure, accessible via the springs that are located on those ledges.
  • The Automatic Ring Panels are located in a corner on the right side of the main structure. From the bases, the corners can be seen in front and to the right.
  • The Explosion Ring Panels are located on the ground in the front of the bases.
  • The Scatter Ring Panels are located on the left of the pillars that are opposite to the bases.
  • The Grenade Ring Panels are located on the top layer of the stage, on a small extension lined with bushes.

Monitors

  • The SRM is located on the lowest layer in the center of the stage. Its base item is a Force Shield.
  • One WRM each is located on the outer edge of top layer of the stage, next to the pillar with the Scatter Ring Panel on it. Its base item is a Super Ring Monitor. The WRM is flanked by two regular ring monitors.
  • One WRM each is located between the springs on the ledges that extend to the left of the main structure and also hold the Rail Ring Panels. Its base item is a Super Ring Monitor.
