MAPF2, Silver Cascade Zone, is the third Capture the Flag level in Sonic Robo Blast 2 v2.2. It is a very open, rectangular stage, consisting of massive concrete bases over a central water pit. The flag rooms are hidden behind waterfalls. The stage was first featured in v1.09, but was re-textured for v2.0, received additional decorations in v2.1 and revamped in v2.2.14.

Points of interest

Weapon rings

  • The Bounce Ring Panels are located on a small ledge to either side of the center pit. They can only be reached from above.
  • The Rail Ring Panels are located under the waterfall of the base entrances.
  • The Automatic Ring Panels are located on a slightly lowered platform in the center pit, just outside the entrance to the underwater tunnels.
  • The Explosion Ring Panels are located on the right side of the pool in the lower part of each base.
  • The Scatter Ring Panels are located on a platform on the right side of the lower part of each base.
  • The Grenade Ring Panels are located in the pool in the upper part of each base, in front of the base entrance.

Monitors

  • The SRM is located on a pillar in the center of the stage, just above the water level. Its base item is a Whirlwind Shield. Its position allows player to bounce on it to quickly reach the other base.
  • One WRM each is located next to the platform that holds the Scatter Ring Panel. Its base item is a Super Ring Monitor.
  • One WRM each is located behind the left pillar in the lower part of each base. Its base item is a Super Ring Monitor.
